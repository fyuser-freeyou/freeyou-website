Logo Usage Rules

✅ Use the saffron mark on navy/dark backgrounds — highest visibility

✅ Use the dark mark version on white/light backgrounds

✅ Minimum size: 24px height for the mark, 80px for the horizontal lockup

✅ Keep clear space equal to the height of the "F" on all sides

❌ Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo

❌ Do not use on busy photographic backgrounds without an overlay

❌ Do not recreate the logo in different fonts — always use the SVG file

❌ Do not apply drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the mark

🎨 Prompt for Ideogram.ai / Midjourney (Logo Generation)

Professional SaaS company logo for "FreeYou", a freelance platform for independent professionals in India. Design a clean vector logomark in Zoho / Freshworks / Razorpay style — geometric, minimal, fully scalable. The mark should be an interlocked "FY" monogram in a rounded square container, OR an abstract geometric person with arms open wide suggesting freedom. Primary color: deep saffron #F5A623 on navy #0A1628. Secondary accent: teal #00C9A7. Wordmark in geometric sans-serif (Sora or Nunito Bold style). The logo must work perfectly at 16x16 favicon size AND full size 400px. Style: flat vector, zero gradients in the primary mark, professional B2B SaaS, South Asian startup aesthetic. Provide horizontal lockup (mark + wordmark) on dark background, on white background, and the isolated mark alone. Clean white background version also. No 3D, no shadows, no complex illustration.
